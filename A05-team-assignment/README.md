## Project: CareerKiosk

**CareerKiosk** is a fully functional, AI-powered career guidance and job matching platform with three main portals:

### 1. Job Seeker Portal
- AI Resume Builder with optimization
- AI Interview Coach with mock sessions and scoring
- Smart Job Matching using AI algorithms
- Application tracker and career goal tracking

### 2. Company Portal (Stanley Huynh)
- Employer dashboard and job posting management
- Hiring pipeline with AI candidate ranking
- Offer management and recruitment analytics

### 3. Admin Portal (Duc Nguyen)
- Platform user management and moderation
- A/B testing engine and analytics
- Comprehensive audit logging
