## Lab Overview

Lab 03 was an individual lab assignment deepening understanding of AI applications in business contexts through hands-on experimentation.

## Objectives

- Apply AI tools to real business scenarios
- Evaluate AI output quality and accuracy
- Develop prompt engineering skills
- Document AI tool workflows for professional use

## Activities

### AI Tool Exploration
- Generative AI for business writing and documentation
- AI data analysis and summarization tools
- AI-assisted code generation and debugging
- Conversational AI for business communication

### Practical Applications
- Used AI to draft and refine business communications
- Applied AI to analyze datasets and extract insights
- Leveraged AI for technical documentation and reporting

## Key Findings

- AI tools significantly reduce time on repetitive tasks
- Effective prompt engineering maximizes AI output quality
- AI outputs require human review for professional standards
- Different platforms excel at different task types

## Lessons Learned

1. Clear, specific prompt engineering is essential
2. Iterative refinement produces better AI outputs
3. Understanding AI limitations prevents over-reliance
4. AI integration into professional workflows must be responsible
